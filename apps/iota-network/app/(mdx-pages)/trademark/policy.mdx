## 1. General

This Trademark Policy sets out guidelines for how to use the IOTA trademarks when referring to projects in the IOTA ecosystem. The aim is to maintain the distinctiveness of the trademarks while also encouraging everyone to build new projects in the IOTA ecosystem.

Please review this Trademark Policy on a regular basis to keep up-to-date with any changes. We also reserve the right to revoke any permission and to change or restrict any use of the trademarks if we believe that such use is in violation of this policy or is harmful to the reputation of the IOTA ecosystem.

## 3. Official trademarks of the IOTA Foundation

IOTA and the IOTA logo are trademarks of the IOTA Foundation. The IOTA Foundation has registered a number of trademarks to protect the names and logos of the main products and services in the IOTA ecosystem. These include IOTA, SHIMMER, SELV and FIREFLY.

You may use these trademarks when referring to the IOTA and Shimmer networks and other official products and services in the IOTA ecosystem. However, please follow the guidelines below when using these official trademarks.

## 3.2 General rules for using the official trademarks

We encourage you to build your project as part of the IOTA ecosystem and we welcome you to use the official trademarks when referring to the IOTA network and other related technologies. Feel free to highlight that your project is built on the IOTA network or that you support the IOTA ecosystem. However, when choosing a name for your project, avoid giving the impression that your project has been endorsed by the IOTA Foundation or that it is part of the services or products offered by the IOTA Foundation. Be creative and use a name and logo that distinguishes your own project from other projects.

You can use the trademarks in both commercial (for-profit) and non-commercial (non-profit) projects, provided that your use of the trademarks does not in any way suggest that your project is the IOTA network or the IOTA token, or an official product or service offered by the IOTA Foundation.

### 3.2.1 General rules for using the official trademarks

If you are building a technical project, you may use the official trademarks to show that your project is built on the IOTA network when you are referring to the IOTA technology, the IOTA token or other official products or services provided by the IOTA Foundation.

Always remember to:

-   Add a link to the official IOTA website [https://www.iota.org](https://www.iota.org) and add a trademark notice to show that these are trademarks of the IOTA Foundation.
-   Follow the trademark guidelines above and the brand style guide, as updated from time to time.

### 3.2.1 Other community projects

You can use the “IOTA Community” logo if you wish to support the IOTA ecosystem and spread awareness about IOTA and Shimmer, or you can use the official trademarks when referring to official networks and tokens or the technical products and services provided by the IOTA Foundation.

For example:

-   Creating a fan page or other information channel for the purpose of educating users about the IOTA network, stimulating developer or researcher discussions, and/or generally raising awareness.
-   Printing IOTA t-shirts, hats and other branded merchandise to be given away or sold in order to spread awareness about the IOTA ecosystem.
-   Designing and distributing marketing materials to advertise meet-ups, hackathons, conferences, developer workshops or other similar events.
-   Using the trademarks in articles and news about the IOTA ecosystem. We welcome you to write news articles, social media posts, or technical reports that discuss different projects in the IOTA ecosystem.
-   Cryptocurrency exchanges and other entities using the trademarks for activities involving commercial or non-commercial offerings of IOTA products, such as the IOTA token.

However, please remember to follow the brand guidelines and this Trademark Policy and to add a trademark notice to show that these are trademarks of the IOTA Foundation. Don’t use the trademarks in a way that suggests that you are an official partner of the IOTA Foundation unless we have agreed to that separately.

## 3.3 What to avoid

In any case, please:

-   Do not use any of the trademarks in any way that could give a false impression that your project is the IOTA network or the IOTA token, or that it has been created or endorsed by the IOTA Foundation.
-   Do not use the trademarks as the name or logo of your own project, application or company.
-   Do not use modified versions of the trademarks. Maintaining a consistent brand appearance helps everyone recognize the IOTA ecosystem.
